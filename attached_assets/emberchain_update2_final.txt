[size=16pt][b]🔥 Forge Community — Live Chat & Forum (FLAGSHIP)[/b][/size]


This is the feature we've been most excited about.

Every EMBR wallet holder now has access to a real-time community space, accessible directly from the wallet sidebar — [b]no separate account, no email, no sign-up.[/b]

[size=14pt][b]💬 Live Chat[/b][/size]

The chat room is live and persistent.

Privacy and user control were priorities.

[size=16pt][b]⛏ Multi-Core Browser Mining[/b][/size]


Multi-core browser mining shipped in the last sprint and continues to perform well.

The EMBR wallet automatically launches:

[list]
[*]One Web Worker per logical CPU core
[*]Independent nonce ranges per worker
[*]Parallel mining across available CPU resources
[/list]

Modern machines receive a significant throughput improvement.

Work templates are fetched centrally and distributed across cores.

The best result from any core triggers share submission.

Stale templates caused by newly discovered blocks are automatically detected, refreshed, and retried.

[size=14pt][b]⚔️ PvP Mining — Competitive Block Rounds[/b][/size]

Mining on EMBR isn't passive background work — every block is a live competition.

During each block window, miners race to submit valid proof-of-work shares.

When the block closes, [b]rewards are split proportionally[/b] across all participants based on the shares they contributed in that round.

Work harder, submit more shares, earn more EMBR.

Coming alongside this is a [b]live mining-pool leaderboard[/b] — showing each miner's rank, share count, and estimated cut of the current block in real time.

[list]
[*]See exactly where you rank against every other active miner
[*]Watch the leaderboard shift as shares are submitted
[*]Track your estimated reward update live as the block progresses
[/list]

If you're running multiple cores you'll feel the difference immediately.

[size=14pt][b]📉 Progressive Halving[/b][/size]

Bitcoin's halving model is well known, but the sudden cliff creates predictable market stress on halving day.

EMBR takes a different approach.

Instead of reward dropping by 50% overnight, the block reward will [b]decrease gradually and continuously[/b] over a defined supply schedule.

[list]
[*]No single halving event for traders to front-run
[*]Smooth, predictable emissions curve over time
[*]Early miners earn more — later miners earn less — without market shocks
[/list]

The result is a fairer, more stable issuance model that rewards early participation without punishing late entrants with a sudden cliff.


[size=14pt][b]🌊 Elastic Issuance[/b][/size]

On top of progressive halving, EMBR will implement an elastic issuance layer.

Block rewards will adjust dynamically based on real network conditions:

[list]
[*]High network activity → rewards expand slightly to keep miners incentivized
[*]Low network activity → rewards contract, controlling supply growth
[*]Target emission rate maintained automatically without hard forks or manual intervention
[/list]

The goal is a chain that breathes with its user base — not one that punishes miners during slow periods or floods supply during boom times.

This mechanism, combined with progressive halving, gives EMBR one of the most considered issuance models in the browser-mining space.
