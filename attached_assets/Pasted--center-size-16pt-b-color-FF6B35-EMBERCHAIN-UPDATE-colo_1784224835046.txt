[b][size=13pt][color=#FF6B35]▌ PROPORTIONAL SHARE POOL — NOW LIVE[/color][/size][/b]
[b]The biggest change: block rewards are no longer winner-takes-all. [/b]
Traditional PoW gives 100% of the block reward to whoever finds the winning nonce. Emberchain now splits it proportionally across every miner who did work that round. Small miners earn steadily. Big miners earn exactly what their hash rate deserves. Nobody takes a cut.
[b]How it works:[/b]
[list]
[li]The mining template now returns two targets — [b]target[/b] (full block) and [b]shareTarget[/b] (64× easier)[/li]
[li]Every valid share hash you submit during a round is recorded against your address[/li]
[li]When any miner finds the winning block, the 5 EMBR reward is split proportionally by share count[/li]
[li]If you submitted 30 out of 60 total shares that round, you receive 2.5 EMBR — regardless of who found the block[/li]
[li]Zero pool fee. Every wei of the block reward goes to miners[/li]
[li]Payout breakdown per miner is stored on each block and visible in the explorer[/li]
[/list]
[b]What changed for external miners:[/b]
[list]
[li]One new endpoint: [b]POST /api/mining/share[/b] — call it whenever your hash meets shareTarget[/li]
[li]That's it. The submit endpoint for winning blocks is unchanged[/li]
[/list]
The updated Python script in the OP handles share submission automatically.

[b][size=13pt][color=#FF6B35]▌ SHARE POOL IS CHEAT-RESISTANT[/color][/size][/b]
A few things that were confirmed with automated tests before shipping:
[list]
[li][b]Wrong difficulty rejected[/b] — a share hash that only meets a lower difficulty than the current chain target is refused[/li]
[li][b]Stale work rejected[/b] — a header with the wrong parentHash or wrong block number is refused even if the hash is valid[/li]
[li][b]Duplicate nonces rejected[/b] — submitting the same nonce twice in a round earns exactly one share, not two[/li]
[li][b]Restarts don't reset the nonce set[/b] — the server persists submitted nonces to disk; you can't replay a nonce by timing a restart[/li]
[li][b]Shares survive restarts mid-round[/b] — if the server restarts before a block is found, your accumulated shares carry forward into the payout[/li]
[li][b]Dust is handled correctly[/b] — rounding remainder always goes to the last miner in the list; total payouts always equal exactly 5 EMBR[/li]
[/list]
